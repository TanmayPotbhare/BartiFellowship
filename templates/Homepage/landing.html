About The Institution

Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research and Training Institute (BARTI), Pune

To realize the vision of the architect of the Constitution of India, Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar; To achieve the objectives enshrined in the Constitution of India viz. Justice, Liberty, Equality, Fraternity among the peoples of our nation; To uplift the disadvantaged sections of the society, from the vortex of blind faith, superstition, communal hatred, caste bias, To obliterate disparity based on sects or gender and develop brotherhood, Scientific temperament and Communal harmony, the Government of Maharashtra in Cultural Affairs, Sports & Tourism Department, vide No. UTA,

Criteria

Scheme Benefits

Junior Research Fellowhsip will get Rs. 31,000/- each Quarter for 2 Years.

Senior Research Fellowhsip will get Rs. 35,000/- each Quarter for 3 Years.

As per revised fellowship amount JRF is 37,000/- and SRF is 42,000/-

Contingency for JRF will be 10,000/- and 12,000/- for Social Science and Science Respectively.

Contingency for SRF will be 20,500/- and 25,000/- for Social Science and Science Respectively.

Escort Reader for Disabled candidate will be 2000/- per month.

Eligibility Criteria

The applicant should be a resident of the state of Maharashtra.

It is necessary for the community of the applicant to be included in the list of Scheduled Caste in the state of Maharashtra.

For this scheme, it is mandatory for the applicant to have a valid Caste certificate and Validity Certificate of scheduled Caste at the time of applying.
